Welcome to Sa Pa

In this wonderful place you will forget all the pressure or stress that modern life brings to you. It will be a rare quiet moment that help you to recall your value, to find yourself again, all things you might lose in busy city life.

Sapa is about 1500m high above the sea level and located 376km to the north of Hanoi.

Here lives 6 folks: Kinh, H'Mong, Dao do, Tay, Giay, Xa Pho, every folks have their own unique tradition.

Cutural Highlight and People

In spite of living with subsistence agricultural, life in Sapa is simple and happy. It is quiet, peaceful and harmony with nature. People in here are hospitable and estimable. They have little to worry about, thus, they live happily and establish a strong bond with others in their community.

There are many festivals most of them are held monthly such as love market (where young people play music to find their lovers).


Nature and Scenic of Beauties

The nature of Sapa is pristine, covered by forest, terraces, watterfall and mist. In Sapa tourist can enjoy fresh atmosphere and wonderful weather. The most special thing about the weather of Sapa is that tourist can experient 4 seasons in one day.
