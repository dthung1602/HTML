Careers at Savitar Travel

Savitar Travel is the world's longest-established travel company, offering high quality group tours, private journeys and tailor-made holidays to many of the world's most fascinating regions. Our luxury holidays range from the lavish to the adventurous, and are planned by experts. We also run a range of culinary holidays under the MasterChef Travel brand.

Our vacancies

We currently have the following roles available. For new roles, please check this page later or connect with us on LinkedIn to know whenever we post new career opportunities.

Sales Manager

We are looking for an experienced Sales Manager to join the team in our central London office. Reporting to the UK Director, this senior role is responsible for the overall performance of our Sales Team. Due to the nature of this role the Sales Manager is also involved in many areas of the business.

IT Assistant

A vacancy has arisen for an IT Assistant to join the team in our central London Office. This is a full time position and will be responsible for IT support consisting of enterprise systems, networks and workplace computing environments. The IT Assistant will work under the guidance of the global IT (infrastructure) management team operating from Savitar Travel India. This role will report to the IT Manager (UK). This position has no direct reports and no management responsibilities.

Tour Consultant - Africa & The Indian Ocean

An opportunity has arisen for a Tour Consultant to join our sales team. The position will include selling brochure tours as well as designing and costing tailor-made itineraries for independent travel to Africa & The Indian Ocean. The ideal candidate will have an excellent knowledge of the regions gained by relevant travel experience along with a passion to sell the destinations.

Good sales techniques and an excellent telephone manner are required. The position requires attention to detail, the ability to meet tight deadlines and work under pressure whilst maintaining a focus on excellent customer service.
