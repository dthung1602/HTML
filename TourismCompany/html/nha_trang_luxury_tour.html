Overview

Evason Hideaway Nha Trang really deserved with the name Paradise on Earth with great combination between beautiful natures, perfect private beaches as well as comfortable accommodation and facilities. You can either to spend time together in the resort, attending some water sport activities or just totally relax with the peaceful atmosphere and professional staff for the spa treatment at famous Six Senses Spa.


Schedule

Day 1: Saigon – Nha Trang - Evasion Hideaway

Morning transfer to Tan Son Nhat airport for the flight to Cam Ranh. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to Evason Hideaway resort - the paradise on Earth.

Overnight at Evason Hideaway Resort.

Day 2: Evason Hideaway (B)

Relaxing at resort. Overnight at Evason Hideaway Resort.

Day 3: Evason Hideaway – Ana Mandara Resort (Nha Trang) (B)

This morning you will be free at leisure before our private car will pick you up at the resort to transfer to Nha Trang - the dynamic sea town. Upon arrival to Nha Trang, check in Ana Mandara resort. Afternoon will be free at leisure for you to relax at Ana Mandara Resort.

Overnight at Ana Mandara Resort.

Day 4: Nha Trang (B)

Morning depart for an excursion out to the bay of Nhatrang, one of 28 best bays in the world. Our first stop is at Mun Island, one of the reserved islands in the area. You can spend time for swimming, snorkeling or take a ride on the glass-bottom boat to explore the beautiful underwater life. Nha Trang is the best place in Vietnam for coral, different species of sea creature and crystal blue water. Then we will have lunch on Tam Island, relax on the beach or experience some water sports like para sailing, canoeing, jet-ski. On the way back, we visit Tri Nguyen Aquarium where you can see some sea creatures you might have not known before take a walk over fishing village on Mieu Island.

Overnight at Ana Mandara Resort.

Day 5: Nha Trang - Saigon (B)

You have some free time in the morning for you to do some shopping, relax on the private beach of the resort before check out the hotel and transferred to Cam Ranh airport. Take the flight back to Saigon. Tour ends.
