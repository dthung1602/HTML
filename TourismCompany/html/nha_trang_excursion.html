Overview

Nha Trang, the city located by one of the top 29 most beautiful bays in the world, with miles of white sandy beaches, swaying coconut palms and wonderful fresh seafood restaurants will atract tourists everywhere.


Schedule

Day 1: Saigon – Nha Trang by Flight

Transfer to airport by yourself to take the flight to Nha Trang in the morning. Tickets provided by Gray Line Vietnam. Upon arrival in Nha Trang, you will be picked-up by your guide and driver to transfer to the hotel to check-in. Time is for your own leisure. Lunch and dinner are at your own account for flexibility. Overnight in Nha Trang.

Day 2: Nha Trang – Boat trip to the island (B,L)

Pick-up at your hotel and transfer to Cau Da Port, visit Tri Nguyen Aquarium at Hon Mieu Island, with many fishes of Nha Trang and Khanh Hoa province which make a small ocean world here. Hon Mun Island is next place in the trip, the best island for snorkeling where you can see lots of colorful lives coral reeves and fishes. Visit a fishing village and having a fresh lunch at the island. On the way back you will be able to see caves in which the sea swallows make their nests. Cruise back to the mainland late afternoon.

Day 3: Nha Trang – Saigon by Flight (B)

At your leisure until transfer by the driver to the airport for your departure flight.
