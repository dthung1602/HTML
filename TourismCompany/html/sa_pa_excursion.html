Overview

Exploring Sapa the most beautiful and peacful valley in the north of Vietnam. You will be impress by greatness of the green terraces, waterfall, campfire,...You can also make friend with local folks, live with them and learn their unique cultural.


Schedule

Day 1: Travel to Sapa at Hanoi train station by train

All tourists will join the group at the Hanoi Train Station. Group take the tickets to the carbins and stay overnight in 6-soft-bed A/C carbins on train to SaPa.

Day 2: Visit Laocai Province and meet local people

After breakfast at hotel, group check out of hotel and transfers to Lao Chai and go trekking to Ta Van, visit Rattan Bridge. Group continues the trail through bamboo forests to Giang Ta Chai waterfall, group treks crossing over a rattan suspension bridge and enter into Giang Ta Chai village which is inhabited by the Red Zao minorities.

Group have lunch either with an ethnic minority family or on the way back to Lao Cai.

Dinner at restaurant in Lao Cai. Group takes the tickets to the cabins and stay overnight in 6-soft-bed A/C carbins on train to Hanoi.

Day 3: Return to Hanoi

Train arrives Hanoi train station at around 06.00 - 07.00. Tour ends.
