Overview

The most time on trip for relaxing on beach in Nha Trang, discovering colorful corals in Nha Trang by private boat trip to Mun Island, where to explore the sea by ocean activities as swimming and snorkeling and scuba diving as well. Enjoy a wonderful time in Vinpearlland Amusement Park for entertainment.


Schedule

Day 1: Nha Trang Arrival

Our driver will pick you up at the airport with your name on board. Then transfer you to hotel in Nha Trang city. Lunch & Dinner at your account.

Overnight in Nha Trang city.

Day 2: Monkey Island & Doc Let Beach (B,L)

Monkey Island – Lao Island - Orchid Spring – Hang Tien Beach – Funny animal Show – Doc Lech Beach

You have a full day tour of visiting the interesting Monkey island on Nha Phu Bay & relaxing at the beautiful Doc Lech Beach. Next to Nha Trang bay, Nha Phu bay creates another attraction to tourists. Monkey islands with wild animals, Orchid spring, Hang Tien beach, coral reef are the places we will go sightseeing. Do not hesitate to make friends with hundreds of lovely monkeys on Hon Lao island, and do not miss the funny animal show, play with elephants and ostriches. You really have precious time of relaxation. Come and join us.

You can go on ahead to Doc Lech Beach after your visit to Monkey islands on tour. Doc Lech Beach with really white sand is 55 Km away from Nhatrang city on the North national road, where you can deep yourselves full day on the most of beautiful beach of Vietnam and enjoy wide range of choice of seafood there with cheap price. You will find a lot of domestic visitors there during Summer time in Nhatrang, properly at peak time in June & July within the year. Dinner at your account.

Overnight in Nha Trang city.

Day 3: Discover the Vinpearlland Amusement Park (B,L)

Discover this exciting amusement park, Vinpearlland by the cable cars over Nhatrang Bay with the longest distance in the world. It takes about 10 minutes by cable car to the park. Start from your hotel at 9.00 am, our car & guide meets & transfers you to Phu Quy Tourism Pier, where you can take the cable car to the Vinpearlland Amusement Park. Then you can spend whole day participating & enjoying all attractive, exciting activities & games there such as Compact coaster, Swing Carousel, Evolution, Merry-go-round, 4D Movies, Bumpers Cars, Crazy Bull riding, all 3D & Video games, Children Paradise & Kid Garden, Newly opened water park & Waterfall Music at night. You can choose either lunch or dinner, which are all included in the tour package. Tour ends around 5.00 pm or at own time. Car & guide will transfer you back to the hotel. Please discover & try once at this exciting park ! Dinner at your account.

Overnight in Nha Trang city.

Day 4: : Nha Trang relax (B)

After breakfast, you will get a day to relax on Nha Trang beach.

Overnight in Nha Trang city

Day 5: Nha Trang Departure (B)

After breakfast, you will get free time until your flight depart. Our driver will pick off you 2.5 hours before departure time.

Tour end here.
