Schedule

Day 1: Ha Long Arrival

Our driver will pick you up at the airport with your name on board. Then transfer you to hotel in Ha Long city. Lunch & Dinner at your account.

Overnight in Ha Long city.

Day 2: Visit Red River Delta

After pick-up at your hotel at 8am, the bus ride through the Red River delta is a great opportunity to see what daily life is like for Vietnamese farmers. Rice fields and water buffalo flank both sides of the road. The most interesting glimpses into rural life come during February and June, when whole families are busy transplanting the young rice plants to the growing fields.

You have a full day tour of visiting the interesting Monkey island on Nha Phu Bay & relaxing at the beautiful Doc Lech Beach. Next to Ha Long bay, Nha Phu bay creates another attraction to tourists. Monkey islands with wild animals, Orchid spring, Hang Tien beach, coral reef are the places we will go sightseeing. Do not hesitate to make friends with hundreds of lovely monkeys on Hon Lao island, and do not miss the funny animal show, play with elephants and ostriches. You really have precious time of relaxation. Come and join us.

Arrive in Halong City tourist wharf around 12pm. Here you'll board a traditional wooden junk and head into the bay. A fresh seafood lunch (vegetarian available if requested) is served on the way out.

Next is a 1.5-hour cooking demonstration with traditional Vietnamese food on the sundeck (or in the restaurant in case of inclement weather) before sitting down to your set-menu dinner in the restaurant. After eating, enjoy free time for squid fishing at reception or relaxing at the bar.

Day 3: Discover Halong Bay (B,L)

The limestone bedrock and sheer cliffs of the countless islets have inspired local people to come up with equally fanciful names: Stone Dogs Island, Duck Island, Finger Island, Incense Burner Island, and Fighting Cocks Island, the symbol of Halong Bay.

Tour end here.
