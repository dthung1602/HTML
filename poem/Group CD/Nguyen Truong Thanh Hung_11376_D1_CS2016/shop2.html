PRINCESS SHOKUSHI

(?-1201)

HOMEPAGE BIOGRAPHY POET 1 > POET 2 POET 3


SHINKOKINSHŪ II: 137



Sent to Prince Kore’akira with a sprig of double-flowered cherry blossom she had had picked. [1]

a) Kanji:

やへにほふ
のきばのさくら
うつろひぬ
風よりさきに
とふ人もがな		 b) Romanji:

Yae niou
nokiba no sakura
utsuroinu
kaze yori saki ni
tou hito mo gana		 c) English:

The double hues
Of cherry beneath my eaves
Have paled;
Before the wind,
I would that you had visited... [2]
