PRINCESS SHOKUSHI

(?-1201)

HOMEPAGE BIOGRAPHY POET 1 POET 2 > POET 3


SHINKOKINSHŪ XX: 1969



In a hundred poem sequence, on the spirit of calming the heart every day in the early morning. [1]

a) Kanji:

しづかなる
あか月ごとに
見わたせば
まだふかきよの
夢ぞかなしき		 b) Romanji:

Shizukanaru
akatsuki goto ni
miwataseba
mada fukaki yo no
yume zo kanashiki		 c) English:

How peaceful is
Every single dawn;
I gaze out, yet
I gaze out, yet
I dream–a grief, indeed. [2]


References:
